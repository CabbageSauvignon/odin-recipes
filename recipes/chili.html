Chili Recipe

Description

This spicy hot chili recipe will leave your taste buds on fire and your stomach full.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef, 85/15 is ideal.
  • 1 white onion, large
  • 6 jalapeno peppers, more if desired
  • 5 habenero peppers
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 can diced tomato
  • 12 oz lager beer
  • 2 tsp cocoa powder
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • salt
  • pepper
  • paprika, preferrably smoked
  • ground cayenne pepper
  • red pepper flakes
  • 1 cup crushed corn chips
  • fresh minced garlic, to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large pot to medium-high heat. Brown the beef
  2. While the beef browns, dice the onion and peppers. Add to the pot and allow to soften.
  3. When the onions and peppers are sauted, add the garlic. After about 30 seconds, add the dried spices
  4. Once the dried spices become fragrant, add the beer and allow to come to a boil.
  5. Add the diced tomatoes. Allow the chili to simmer for at least an hour before serving.
